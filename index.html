About Me

I've always had a fascination with technology. In late 2023, I took a leap and began teaching myself coding to fulfill my creative urge to build. While I enjoyed the challenges and rewards of optometry, I craved a new way to channel my creativity. I discovered free online courses like CS50x and The Odin Project, and I was immediately captivated. Coding felt like electronic Lego—assembling pieces to create something functional and dynamic was incredibly satisfying.

In 2024, my passion led me to enroll in a graduate certificate program at Deakin University, where I further developed my skills in web and game development. I'm proud to share my journey through my projects, which are listed from oldest to newest. Currently, I'm redesigning a website for a local pharmacy using Tailwind and React. Additionally, I'm diving into the challenge of creating a custom web-based dispensing system to address the shortcomings of the current software. I'm excited about the new skills and knowledge this journey will bring.
